SINGAPPORE STUDY TOUR

The primary objective of this four-day senior high school study tour was to provide students with a multifaceted look at Singapore’s global success, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world application. By exploring the architectural marvels of Nanyang Technological University (NTU) and the sustainable urban design of Gardens by the Bay, students analyzed the integration of STEM and green technology in a modern metropolis. From the cultural heritage of Chinatown to the world-class tourism infrastructure of Sentosa Island and Jewel Changi Airport, the journey was designed to foster global citizenship, financial literacy, and an appreciation for how innovation and multiculturalism drive a nation’s economic growth. FIRST DAY, 11TH APRIL

Welcome to Singapore! Your journey begins in the heart of the city at Marina Bay, where futuristic architecture meets the sea. Start your day with a classic photo at Merlion Park before crossing the iconic Helix Bridge to the Singapore Flyer for a breathtaking panoramic view of the skyline. Wrap up your afternoon at the lotus-inspired ArtScience Museum, immersing yourself in the world of digital art and innovation. It’s a high-energy introduction to the Lion City that perfectly blends culture, height, and high-tech wonder.

Learn more

SECOND DAY, 12TH APRIL

On the second day, your journey takes you to the western edge of Singapore to explore the stunning campus of Nanyang Technological University (NTU). A must-see is The Hive, famously nicknamed the "Dim Sum Basket" building for its unique stacked-tower design. After marveling at the eco-friendly architecture and the lush Yunnan Garden, make your way south to Sentosa Island. Transition from academic inspiration to island relaxation as you spend the afternoon lounging on the sands of Siloso Beach, catching a thrill at the Skyline Luge, or exploring the immersive Sentosa Sensoryscape.

Learn more

THIRD DAY, 13TH APRIL

On Day 3, your mission was all about finding the perfect gifts to take home. You dove into the vibrant cultural heart of Chinatown and the high-energy stalls of Bugis Street, two of the best places in Singapore to score a bargain. In Chinatown, the streets were a treasure trove of heritage. You likely found beautiful silk scarves, traditional Chinese tea sets, and those classic Merlion statuettes that serve as the ultimate memento. The red lanterns and historic shophouses provided a stunning backdrop as you hunted for intricate handicrafts.

Learn more

FOURTH DAY, 14TH APRIL

On our final day, you saved the best for last, transitioning from the futuristic nature of Gardens by the Bay to the architectural masterpiece of Jewel Changi Airport before heading home.

Merlion Park

Visit the iconic Merlion Park, where the mythical creature stands proudly in front of the Singapore River. This landmark is a perfect spot for photos and a symbol of Singapore's identity.

ArtScience Museum

Sentosa Island

Our adventure continued to Sentosa Island, where excitement filled every corner of the day. From exploring the vibrant attractions to experiencing the thrilling rides at Universal Studios Singapore, every moment was unforgettable. Surrounded by laughter, adrenaline, and iconic movie-themed zones, we created memories that will stay with us long after the journey ends.
